Bedrock Redstone Interactive

Build and simulate 3D bedrock redstone circuits with stacked blocks, pistons, observers, and comparator logic. Use mouse to orbit camera, left-click to place, right-click to remove. Drag to rotate components.

Controls
  • Mouse drag to orbit camera around the grid.
  • Left click on a block to place the selected component on top.
  • Right click on a block to remove the top component.
  • Shift + click to rotate direction-aware components.
Learning goal

Use levers as input power, dust to transport current, repeaters to direct signals, torches as inverters, and lamps to show outputs.
